Understanding Hair Loss

Hair loss affects millions globally. Learn about the common causes and what you can do about them.

Genetic Factors (Androgenetic Alopecia)

Pattern baldness accounts for 95% of hair loss. Understand how DHT sensitivity affects hair follicles.

Stress-Related Hair Loss (Telogen Effluvium)

Psychological and physical stress can push hair into the shedding phase prematurely.

Nutritional Deficiencies

Lack of iron, zinc, B vitamins, and protein can lead to hair thinning and loss.

Scalp Conditions

Dandruff, psoriasis, and fungal infections can damage hair follicles and cause hair loss.

Hormonal Changes

Thyroid disorders, PCOS, and hormonal imbalances significantly affect hair health.

Lifestyle Factors

Poor diet, lack of sleep, smoking, and excessive heat styling damage hair over time.

Hair Loss Solutions

Preventive Care

  • Gentle handling and proper brushing techniques
  • Minimize heat styling and chemical treatments
  • Use sulfate-free, pH-balanced shampoos
  • Regular scalp massages to improve circulation

Nutritional Support

  • Iron-rich foods (spinach, red meat)
  • Zinc sources (pumpkin seeds, oysters)
  • B-complex vitamins and biotin
  • Protein and omega-3 fatty acids

Medical Treatments

  • Topical minoxidil (Rogaine)
  • Oral finasteride (Propecia)
  • PRP therapy and stem cell treatments
  • Hair transplantation options

Lifestyle Changes

  • Stress management and meditation
  • Regular exercise and quality sleep
  • Quit smoking and limit alcohol
  • Protect hair during sleep
